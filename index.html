Who We Are

Driven by Compassion.
Guided by Purpose.

PawsForward is a non-profit organisation dedicated to the welfare of animals across East Africa. Since 2015, we have operated rescue missions, wildlife rehabilitation programmes, and community education drives that put animal welfare at the heart of everything we do.

Our team of dedicated veterinarians, field officers, and volunteers works around the clock to ensure no animal is left behind — from injured street dogs to displaced wildlife.

What We Do

Our Core Programs

Three pillars that shape every action we take.

Animal Rescue

Our rapid-response rescue teams operate 24/7, reaching animals in distress — from road accident victims to those suffering neglect or abuse.

Support Rescue →

Rehabilitation

Injured and traumatised animals receive comprehensive veterinary care, behavioural therapy, and a safe environment designed for recovery.

Support Rehab →

Adoption & Rehoming

We match rehabilitated animals with vetted, loving homes through our structured adoption programme, complete with post-adoption follow-up.

Find a Pet →

Community Education

We run school outreach drives, public workshops, and digital campaigns that build empathy and responsible animal ownership across communities.

Learn More →

Wildlife Protection

We partner with conservation bodies to protect endangered wildlife from poaching, habitat loss, and human-animal conflict across the region.

Support Wildlife →

Veterinary Aid

Our mobile veterinary clinics provide free medical services including vaccinations, sterilisation, and emergency treatment in underserved areas.
